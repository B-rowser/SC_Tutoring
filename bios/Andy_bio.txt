
 Andrew (Andy) Spring is a lifelong Petaluma resident with a deep passion for education and public service. A proud alumnus of Petaluma High School, Andy was a cornerstone of the Trojan Performers Theater program, appearing in eight theatrical productions. He began his higher education at Santa Rosa Junior College before transferring to the University of California, Santa Cruz, where he earned a B.A. in Literature with a concentration in Creative Writing and Poetry, graduating in 2019. Driven by a love for storytelling, Andy later moved to Los Angeles to study screenwriting and acting at the Los Angeles Performing Arts Conservatory (LAPAC), graduating in 2023. Today, he is channeling his diverse artistic and academic background into the classroom, completing his credential through the Sonoma County Office of Education’s "Be a Teacher" intern program. Andy is dedicated to giving back to his local community by helping the next generation of students find their unique voices.
