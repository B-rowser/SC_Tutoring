I'm a science teacher and researcher based in Healdsburg, with a PhD in molecular microbiology and years of hands-on lab experience. Before moving into teaching, I worked as a Principal Scientist in the food science industry and did research at Duke University. I now hold a teaching credential in Biology and Chemistry.
I tutor biology and chemistry at the high school and introductory college level. My goal is to help students actually understand the material, not just memorize it for the next test. I break down hard concepts into plain language and connect them to things that make sense in the real world.
As a former English learner myself, I know what it feels like to work through tough material in a second language. I'm patient, I meet students where they are, and I speak intermediate Spanish if that helps.
